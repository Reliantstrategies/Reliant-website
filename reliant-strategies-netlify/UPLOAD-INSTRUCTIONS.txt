RELIANT STRATEGIES — NETLIFY UPLOAD

1. Sign in to your Netlify account.
2. Open https://app.netlify.com/drop
3. Drag this entire folder (reliant-strategies-netlify) onto the upload area.
4. When publishing finishes, open Site configuration > Change site name.
5. Choose an available name such as reliant-strategies.

Your free address will then look similar to:
https://reliant-strategies.netlify.app

The website contains Home, Services, and Contact pages. The contact form opens
the visitor's email app with a message addressed to reliantstrategies@yahoo.com.
